# Animations

## Purpose

Animations make crossbreeding and feedback readable. They should support learning and pacing, not distract from plant information.

## Germination Bed Animation

Intent:

- Show offspring germination as the center of gameplay.

Behavior:

- Bed cells enter seeded state when crossbreeding starts.
- Seeds grow into seedlings or adult phenotype sprites in a readable sequence.
- Contract-matching cells receive a brief icon/outline pulse.
- Selected specimens receive a persistent focus frame.

Suggested duration:

- 0.25 to 0.5 seconds per bed-cell reveal in normal speed.

## Plant Appearing

Intent:

- Make newly generated offspring feel produced.

Behavior:

- Small pop-in, sprout, grow-in, or bloom motion.
- Trait reveal happens after plant appears.

Suggested duration:

- 0.25 to 0.4 seconds.

## Crossbreeding Start

Intent:

- Confirm parent selection and process start.

Behavior:

- Parent cards pulse or connect with a subtle line.
- Crossbreed button enters active state.

Suggested duration:

- 0.4 to 0.8 seconds.

## Discovery

Intent:

- Celebrate new knowledge.

Behavior:

- Discovery glow around plant.
- Collection icon pulse.
- Popup appears.

Suggested duration:

- 0.6 to 1.2 seconds.

## Contract Completed

Intent:

- Confirm objective success.

Behavior:

- Contract progress fills.
- Reward popup appears.
- Positive sound or visual cue if audio exists.

Suggested duration:

- 0.8 to 1.5 seconds.

## Reduced Motion Rule

If reduced motion is enabled, replace movement with fades, highlights, and clear state changes.
