# Background Assets

## Purpose

Backgrounds establish place and mood while keeping UI readable.

## Main Greenhouse

Usage:

- Main Game Screen.
- Greenhouse Screen.

Direction:

- Bright greenhouse interior.
- Visible benches, glass, soil, and botanical tools.
- Low clutter behind interactive elements.

## Genetic Laboratory

Usage:

- Genetic Analyzer Screen.
- Tutorial genetic explanation moments.

Direction:

- Clean botanical lab.
- Screens, sample trays, labels, and plant science tools.
- Friendly educational tone.

## Market

Usage:

- Contract Screen.

Direction:

- Soft market or request board mood.
- Client-focused but not character-heavy unless clients are later specified.

## Collection Archive

Usage:

- Collection Screen.
- Genetic encyclopedia views.

Direction:

- Organized specimen archive.
- Cards, labels, shelves, notebooks, or botanical catalog feel.

## Shop / Upgrade Area

Usage:

- Shop Screen.

Direction:

- Greenhouse supply area.
- Upgrade tools, shelves, analyzer parts, seed trays.

## Rules

- Backgrounds must not obscure text.
- Keep central interactive zones clean.
- Avoid dark, blurred, or purely atmospheric backgrounds.
- Do not add visual systems that imply unplanned mechanics.
